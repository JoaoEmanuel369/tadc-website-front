About the Show

The Amazing Digital Circus is a surreal, psychological comedy about a bunch of cute cartoon characters that hate their lives and want to leave.

The series follows the adventures of Pomni, a normal woman who unexplainably becomes trapped in a virtual reality with five other humans and are subject to the whims of a wacky AI and their own personal traumas

Debuting in October 2023, the series became an overnight, runaway hit, becoming the most viewed animation pilot on Youtube, amassing hundreds of millions of views within a few months. The show has gone global, with merchandise appearing on retail shelves internationally, being dubbed in over 15 languages, and has dedicated fanbases all over the world.
